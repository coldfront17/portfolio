Mataliki Hana Nui

Role / Type
UX Lead / Heritage
Platform
Mobile
Timeline
2022 - 2023
Location
Aotearoa New Zealand

Identifying a critical need for engaging and accessible digital tools in cultural heritage preservation, I led the UX design for a mobile-based learning experience targeted at enhancing cultural awareness.

The Challenge

Existing digital tools for cultural heritage education were either too generic or too academically dense to resonate with broader audiences. The challenge was to design a mobile experience that made heritage content genuinely engaging, while remaining sensitive to the values and narratives of the communities being represented.

Design Process & Methodology

I conducted extensive user research and leveraged co-design methods to understand the educational needs of the target audience. Based on these insights, I designed a mobile UI/UX that integrated interactive learning modules with culturally sensitive content to ensure inclusivity — iterating through low and high-fidelity prototypes before arriving at the final design.

Outcome

This resulted in a culturally resonant mobile learning prototype that successfully blended educational objectives with engaging digital interactions, paving the way for deeper community engagement with heritage preservation.
