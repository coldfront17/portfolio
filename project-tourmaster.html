Tour Master 360

Role / Type
Designer & Developer / Platform
Platform
Unity / WebXR / 360°
Timeline
2023 - 2024
Location
Aotearoa New Zealand

Addressing the growing demand for immersive tourism platforms that provide accessible remote travel experiences, I designed and developed "Tour Master 360," a comprehensive 360-degree interactive storytelling system. I was tasked with creating a platform that wasn't merely a collection of images but a cohesive narrative experience for virtual exploration. I implemented spatial computing principles and utilized tools like Unity and WebXR for rapid prototyping to craft user-centered narratives.

By managing the end-to-end design process, I ensured seamless cross-platform usability and maintained high levels of user engagement throughout the experience. Ultimately, I delivered a highly immersive platform that successfully demonstrated the significant potential of spatial computing in the tourism sector, offering a novel way for users to explore destinations remotely.

This project solidified my expertise in web-based immersive storytelling, and I am excited to explore how AI-driven content could further personalize these virtual journeys in the future.
