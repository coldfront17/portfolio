Tour Master 360

Role / Type
Designer & Developer / Platform
Platform
Unity / WebXR / 360°
Timeline
2023 - 2024
Location
Aotearoa New Zealand

Addressing the growing demand for immersive tourism platforms that provide accessible remote travel experiences, I designed and developed "Tour Master 360," a comprehensive 360-degree interactive storytelling system.

The Challenge

Existing remote tourism tools lacked the depth and immersion needed to meaningfully convey a sense of place. The challenge was to create a platform that combined high-quality spatial storytelling with cross-platform accessibility, enabling users to explore destinations remotely through an engaging, user-centered experience.

Design Process & Methodology

I implemented spatial computing principles and rapid prototyping using tools like Unity and WebXR to craft user-centered narratives. By managing the end-to-end design process, I ensured seamless cross-platform usability and high user engagement — iterating on navigation patterns, spatial audio cues, and hotspot interactions to optimize the immersive flow.

Outcome

I delivered a highly immersive platform that successfully demonstrated the potential of spatial computing in the tourism sector, creating a novel and engaging way for users to explore destinations remotely. The project has since been recognized as a model for immersive virtual tour creation for Tangata Whenua communities.
