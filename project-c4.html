C4 Coffee Christchurch Order System

Role / Type
UI/UX Designer / Commercial
Platform
Mobile / Web App
Timeline
2022
Location
Christchurch, NZ

C4 Coffee, a prominent local brand in Christchurch, required a modernized digital ordering system to improve customer experience and streamline daily operations.

The Challenge

The existing ordering process created friction for both customers and staff — it was slow, inconsistent across locations, and not optimized for mobile use. C4 Coffee needed a solution that could modernize the customer-facing experience without disrupting established workflows.

Design Process & Methodology

Leading the UI/UX design process, I mapped the customer journey through user research to identify friction points, then created low and high-fidelity wireframes and interactive mockups using Figma. By iterating rapidly based on usability testing and stakeholder feedback, I refined the ordering flow to minimize taps and maximize clarity.

Outcome

I delivered a polished, intuitive UI/UX prototype that optimized the ordering workflow and significantly improved the anticipated user experience — providing C4 Coffee with a clear foundation for their digital transformation.
