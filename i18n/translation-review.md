## 1. Immersive Regenerative Tourism

**标签**：Research · AR / Mobile  
**时间**：2025 - Present

### 英文原文

**标题**：Immersive Regenerative Tourism

**摘要**：With the tourism industry in Aotearoa New Zealand requiring a transformative approach to foster culturally grounded experiences, I joined an NZD $8.2M MBIE-funded multi-university project as a Postdoctoral Researcher.
